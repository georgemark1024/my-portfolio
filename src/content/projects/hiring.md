## Project Overview

In Nairobi, often people have to rely on water vendors to fulfill their water requirements. Fluid Connects aims to serve as a one stop destination for people seeking water deliveries.

## Key Achievements

- Developed in-app messaging for communication between buyer and seller
- Implemented payment processing within the app.
- Buyer and seller rating for quality control

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS
- **Backend**: Django
